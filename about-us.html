About Us

Incepted in the year 1996, Swastika Components Pvt. Ltd. is engaged in the manufacturing and exporting wide range of sintered bushes and parts for a diverse range of industries like automobile, home appliances and many others.

We are an ISO 9001: 2015 certified firm, which began with an aim to provide world class sintered bushes and sintered parts of ferrous and non-ferrous metals. Leveraging on our dedicated team of expert engineers and modern infrastructure we have succeeded in crafting an indelible mark for ourselves.

We are one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of bushes, bronze bushes, bearing bushes, metal bushes, industrial bushes, sintered bushes, sintered iron bushes, sintered bronze bushes, iron sintered bushes, iron sintered parts, automotive bushings and automotive parts with more than 1000 types. Our commitment towards excellence has made us to deliver optimum quality spares that are designed and developed after a thorough analysis of the industry requirement. We conduct rigorous test at each stage of manufacturing to ensure that the end product is in line with the client expectation.

The visionary approach and experience of our founder, Mr. K.K. Sadani, has further helped us to strive and reach at a commendable position.

We "Swastika Components Pvt Ltd" are involved in Manufacturing and Trading a range of Bronze Bushes, Sintered Bushes, Cam Locks, Machine Components, etc.
