About Us

Incepted in the year 1996, Swastika Components Pvt. Ltd. is engaged in the manufacturing and exporting wide range of sintered bushes and parts for a diverse range of industries like automobile, home appliances and many others. An ISO 9001: 2015 certified firm, we are one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of bushes, bronze bushes, bearing bushes, metal bushes, industrial bushes, etc. with more than 1000 types.

Our Mission

Our commitment towards excellence has made us to deliver optimum quality spares that are designed and developed after a thorough analysis of the industry requirement. We conduct rigorous test at each stage of manufacturing to ensure that the end product is in line with the client expectation.

Application Areas

Offering an extensive range of sintered parts such as auto components, auto spare parts, self lubricated bushes, auto bearings, sintered bushes and other such parts, we cater to a large number of clients in varied industries that are:

• Automobile Industry
• Home Appliances, Fans & coolers
• Office Equipment & Printers

• Electric and electronic industry
• Locks & hardware
• Textile machinery

• Jute Industry
• Hand tools & grinders
• Other Industrial requirements
